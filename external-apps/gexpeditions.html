Google Expeditions is an educational app which allows teachers and students explore together the worlds of virtual-reality and augmented-reality. It has over 1000 virtual-reality tours and 100 augmented-reality tours, besides you being able to create your own tour. The app supports the creation of classes, in which a teacher can act like a guide and lead the students locally connected from their smartphones or tablets.

So, are you ready to swim with sharks, visit the outer space, explore the ancient Rome or playing with Dinosaurs?
