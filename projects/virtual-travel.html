Virtual Travel

"Virtual Travel" is a project that aims to provide a trip around the world without you having to leave your home. With a Virtual Reality mode, you can view several incredible places around the world and still find out some curiosities about each one.

More modes


Virtual Reality Full Screen

In order to fully experiece the VR mode, it is required one HMD (head-mounted display).

It may be necessary to accept the installation of Google Virtual Reality services on the first access.
